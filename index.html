CV (Curriculum Vitae)
I am PhD student in Computer Science at Korea Institute of Advanced Science and Technology (KAIST). My research interest pertains to analyzing the impact of input prompt on Large Language Models (LLMs) and identifying the optimal prompts for aligning LLMs under diverse conditions.
